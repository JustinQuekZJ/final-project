NYC Crime and Safety Tool

Instructions:

This is a crime and safety tool for residents in New York City. Users may interact with the map in two ways:

1: Hover over each precinct to view the stop-and-frisk and major crime statistics in that precinct.

2: Enter your current location in the search bar at the top right-hand corner and view the NYPD station in your precinct, along with a route to get there.
